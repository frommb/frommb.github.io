Fitt's Law

Google Inc

Google

Put simply, Fitts's Law states "the time to acquire a target is a function of the distance to and size of the target" Google's main website does a great job at following Fitt's Law. They have condensed all of the information someone would need into a clean space for the purpose of getting someone somewhere else.
