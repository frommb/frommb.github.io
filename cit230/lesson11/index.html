Winter Preparations

Record lows are hitting the Southeast Idaho area at an earlier time this year than usual. Southeast Idaho is expected to have record snowfall this winter. So, it is important that you are prepared early with any snow equipment in your vehicles and in your homes. We suggest having at least 72 hours worth of emergency food, water, light, heating and power sources in case of blackouts from any blizzards or other winter storms that hit the area. We also suggest having new snow tires put on and emergency food, first aid kits and blankets in your vehicles as well. We want to make sure that everyone stays safe this winter.
