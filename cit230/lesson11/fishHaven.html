Fishing in Fish Haven is Heaven

Nestled close to the border of Idaho and Utah is a quaint town called Fish Haven that is located right near Bear lake. Fish Haven is a great place to visit if you love the serene beauty that nature provides. A common activity here in Fish Haven that people enjoy doing year round is fishing. During the summer time people are found catching fish to grill for a barbecue and during the winter time people are out ice fishing trying to catch for a warm winter night's meal. Fishing here in Fish Haven is an activity that is enjoyed by many year round.

Every summer for two weeks, there is a fishing contest to see who can catch the largest fish. The reward that come with this is having the title of best fisher in Fish Haven for that year and having one's picture taken and hung in the town hall next to all the previous winners pictures. The largest fish ever caught in Fish Haven was a rainbow trout that weighed in at 37lbs! You will also find people wakesurfing and jetsking during the summer as well. So, if fishing is not for you, come enjoy a day doing water activities or just come enjoy a day in the sun by the beautiful shimmering blue lake.
